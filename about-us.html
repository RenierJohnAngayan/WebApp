about us


Providing Quality Online Pharmacy in Mindanao


VeerUs was founded in 2019 with a vision of creating an online pharmacy where everyone could have access to quality pharmaceutical care. That vision has grown VeerUs to be one of the most reliable online pharmacy in Davao, serving Samal City and surrounding communities.

We serve adults, children, and provide assistance to elderly. You can rely on VeerUs for routine and emergency treatments. Additionally, we offer Drug Dictionary in order to provide adequate information about the mechanism of action of the drugs and its proper dosing. Our team is committed to providing 24/7 counselling to meet the unique needs of each and every patient. With our expert team of doctors, and pharmacists, you have a full range of pharmaceutical needs covered.

Innovation that helps us better meet the needs of our patients drives us to continually improve our scope of services and our patient experience. Though we've been in the Pharmaceutical industry for two years, we are looking forward to embrace improvisations and seeing your smile in the future!
